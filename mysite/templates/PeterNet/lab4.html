Subdivisions 1 6

INSTRUCTIONS:

-The Subdivisions slider lets you control how many subdivision the gasket has.

-The RotateX, RotateY, and RotateZ buttons rotate the gasket 90 degrees around the gasket's X, Y and Z axes respectively.

-The camera will always look at the gasket.

-Press 'E' to swap between mouse modes. (You can't swap while holding down the mouse)

-Translate Mode:

-Click & Drag to move the gasket up, down, left, and right.

-Press and hold 'W' or 'S' to move the gasket forward or backward.

-Rotate Mode:

-Click & Drag to rotate the gasket.

-Camera Mode:

-Click & Drag to move the camera up, down, left, and right.

-Press and hold 'W' or 'S' to move the camera forward or backward.

-FOV Mode:

-Click & Drag up to decrease the field of view.(10 degree minimum)

-Click & Drag down to increase the field of view.(170 degree maximum)
